Who We Are

Kamate Group Ltd is a Ugandan company established in 2024 with a mission of providing innovative technology and travel solutions to individuals, businesses and organizations.

We believe that technology should be accessible, affordable and impactful. We also understand that travel-related processes can be stressful and time-consuming. That is why we work to simplify both worlds through professional guidance and dependable support.

Whether you need a website, software solution, mobile application, travel documentation assistance, flight booking support or digital transformation services, our team is committed to helping you succeed.
